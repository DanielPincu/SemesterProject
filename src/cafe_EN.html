TRAPHOLT

Entering the Gustav Lind Trapholt feels like a natural extension of your Trapholt experience. Here you can enjoy fine food that focuses on sustainability, organic produce and green thinking.


The menu reflects what’s in season, and you can always be sure of a pleasant dining experience. Of course, we also accommodate those little moments where you simply want to enjoy a glass of wine or indulge in a piece of homemade cake with a cup of coffee.

WEDNESDAY NIGHT SPECIALS


Every Wednesday from 17.30 to 21.00 we serve a delicious seasonal two-course menu. The menu varies from week to week, but will always comprise a starter and a main course. Booking is essential; please book online. When booking, you will also have the opportunity to notify us about any dietary restrictions you may have. Of course, we offer a vegetarian option.
Price: EUR 36,92,-/46,99,- for two/ three courses.


To visit the Gustav Lind Trapholt restaurant, all guests must have a valid ticket to the museum. You can combine your admission fee and the Wednesday night set menu for DKK 340,- / 415,- by booking your table in advance. Your admission fee covers access to the museum from 17.00 to 21.00

SATURDAY & SUNDAY BRUNCH


Gustav Lind Trapholt’s brunch is served to guests arriving between 10.30 and 11.30
The brunch is served directly at the table in three servings, each containing all the delicious dishes you could wish for in a brunch. Of course, organic coffee and juice are included in the price.
Serving brunch this way helps us avoid food waste, allowing us to invest more in local produce and improve sustainability overall.


Minimum order for brunch: four adults.
Brunch incl. drinks – DKK 350
To visit the Gustav Lind Trapholt restaurant, all guests must have a valid ticket to the museum. You can combine your admission fee and your brunch deal for DKK 415 per person by booking your table in advance no later than the night before.
