Inaugurated in 1988, the museum is located in a scenic area overlooking the Kolding Fjord. Our exhibitions present you with innovative, colorful and avant-garde art.

Current Exhibitions

MARIA TORP

Shaping a pattern

In January 2024, Trapholt presents the solo exhibition Shaping a Pattern, where the Danish artist Maria Torp explores the potential of portraiture as an activist and enlightening format. The exhibition will be a sensual and enlightening journey through five years of work, featuring 12 portraits of influential individuals, interviews, and Torp’s research material.

Christmas at Trapholt

Beyond visual delights, the museum resonates with the sounds of seasonal melodies, interactive workshops, and a lively Christmas market featuring local artisans. In the midst of the festivities, Trapholt Museum remains a haven for reflection, offering both exuberant celebration and quiet contemplation. This year, as the museum marks its Christmas milestone, patrons are invited to share in the joy, artistry, and community spirit that define this magical holiday celebration at Trapholt."
