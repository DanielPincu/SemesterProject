MARIA TORP

Shaping a pattern

In January 2024, Trapholt presents the solo exhibition Shaping a Pattern, where the Danish artist Maria Torp explores the potential of portraiture as an activist and enlightening format. The exhibition will be a sensual and enlightening journey through five years of work, featuring 12 portraits of influential individuals, interviews, films, and Torp’s extensive research material. The exhibition will be held from January 18th to November 24th, 2024....READ MORE
